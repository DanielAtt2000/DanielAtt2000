Wheel Alignment

Cost Saving, Improved Handling.

Have you recently had a suspension part replaced? Do you feel your vehicle pulling from side to side while driving straight down the road? Are your tyres wearing out quickly? These are all symptoms of poor wheel alignment. Through our computerized system we ensure that your vehicle is restored to its original alignment measurements, offering:

Better Fuel Economy
Longer Tyre Life
Improved Handling
Increased Safety
